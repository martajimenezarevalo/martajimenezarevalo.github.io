Hello! I’m Maka, an architect trained at ETSAM (Polytechnic University of Madrid), the Technical University of Berlin, and the University of Alicante.
I am currently studying the *Master's Program in Media Design at Keio University in Tokyo (Japan),* with the "La Caixa" Foundation Fellowship.
For the last two years, I have been working at TBA21 — Thyssen Bornemisza Art Contemporary, developing my practice in Collective Learning & Relationships within Organismo | Art in Applied Critical Ecologies, an independent study program developed as an experimental initiative with National Museum Thyssen-Bornemisza. It weaves together research, theory, and speculation through applied case studies to foster regenerative interventions.
In the past, I have collaborated with diverse design and architecture studios in Berlin, Madrid, Alicante, and Valencia, including CuldeSac, Paisaje Transversal, Soriano & Asociados Arquitectos, and Fundbüro. I’ve also served as an Assistant Professor in Architectural Projects at ETSAM and co-founded multidisciplinary collectives such as o.kroma, Grábalos35, and 100x10. In 2018, with Grábalos35 we performed at the opening of the Spanish Pavilion at the Venice Biennale.
I’ve been awarded both the Arquia Foundation Scholarship (2023) and the “la Caixa” Foundation Postgraduate Abroad Fellowship (2024).
